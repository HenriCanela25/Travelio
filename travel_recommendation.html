WHO WE ARE

About us


Travelio was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate travellers who believed that discovering the world should be effortless, inspiring, and deeply personal. We curate the finest destinations and experiences so you can focus on what matters — the journey.

From hidden beach coves in Southeast Asia to the snow-capped peaks of the Alps, our expert team researches every destination to bring you trusted, up-to-date recommendations.
